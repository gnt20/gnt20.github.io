Location

The workshop is held at the École normale supérieure (ENS), 29 rue d'Ulm, 75005 Paris, in the heart of the Latin Quarter. The exact room will be announced closer to the date.


View larger map on OpenStreetMap

Getting there

By public transport

  • RER B — Luxembourg station (5 min walk).
  • Métro line 7 — Place Monge (8 min walk).
  • Métro line 10 — Cardinal Lemoine (10 min walk).
  • Bus — lines 21, 27, 38, 82, 84, 89.

From the airports

  • Paris–Charles de Gaulle (CDG) — RER B direct to Luxembourg (~45 min).
  • Paris–Orly (ORY) — Orlyval + RER B to Luxembourg (~40 min), or tram T7 + métro.

Accommodation

Several hotels are within walking distance of ENS in the 5th and 6th arrondissements. We recommend booking early — June is high season in Paris. A short list of suggestions will be circulated to confirmed participants.
